# softplay — V1.5 status & V2 direction

*Strategic brief. Last updated 2026-06-23. Update as decisions land.*

A planning-level overview for thinking about Stripe, softplay-at-home, and V2 timing.
For the detailed session-by-session changelog see `PROGRESS.md`; for the project briefing see `CLAUDE.md`.

## V1.5 — essentially shipped

**Core infrastructure (done):**
- Magic-link auth + Supabase cloud sync (saves follow you across devices)
- Personalized Free Play (pulls in your saved Playbill)
- **Resend custom email** live — magic links send from `noreply@mysoftplay.app`, no rate limit (ready for 10+ testers)
- Auto-deploy: `git push` → Vercel production

**Recent polish (done):**
- **Date/event intelligence** — typing a date or event ("this weekend", "Bloomsday", "June 30th") triggers a real-time web search for local happenings; "today" is the default and does NOT search; results are radius-bounded and freshness-checked (won't suggest discontinued festivals)
- **Flag persistence fixed** — "Permanently closed" sticks across sessions and both pathways; "Not today"/"Bad suggestion" stay session-only (situational, e.g. no car today)
- **Playground "Play this card" regenerates** date-aware detail on replay (strips stale framing, adds seasonal warnings, fills real address/hours). Convention: the Play On experience must feel identical from both pathways.
- **Non-existent food fixed** — Half Time + itinerary prompts require real, operating venues
- **Confetti** on the winner card (original design), unified wheel/chip colors (one source of truth), readable white chips, clearer save copy across all three pathways, dead-code cleanup (Free Play wheel scaffolding removed)

## Pre-launch / open decisions
- **Pricing** — 2 of 2 Google Form responses pushed back on subscription; casual feedback positive ("I'd definitely use this"). DECISION: wait for ~10 responses before choosing model (subscription vs. one-time vs. freemium / family plan).
- **Stripe** — deliberately POST-beta, not concurrent (decided 2026-06-08). Beta feedback shapes the pricing model first; then Stripe is the beta→launch bridge (a contained 1–2 session job).
- **Migrate original 2 testers** to mysoftplay.app (sign in on softplay-five.vercel.app first to upload saves, then on mysoftplay.app).

## V2 — themes captured (NOT scheduled)
A backlog of directions, not a dated release. Full detail in `CLAUDE.md`.
- **Confirmation/reflection page** (pre-card "let me get this right…"; tiered by familiarity; selective write-back to the Playbill; guardrail = stays a decision tool, not a chatbot) — *pre-wheel, so shareable with at-home*
- **Elevate the Playground** — save Play On add-ons for later, user-seeded "blank" cards (type a place → app fills the card), edit/rearrange/merge saves
- **Around-the-wheel saving** — save from the finalist chips (last pre-spin save point) + post-wheel escape to Playground (bail-to-Playground, NOT save-the-card — the user may dislike the pick)
- **Refine the 3 flag reasons** into real feedback signal (disambiguate "Not today"; capture *why* for "Bad suggestion")
- Email reminders, shareable itinerary links, family voting, playlist, randomness generator

## softplay-at-home (separate product/repo)
- Shares the **pre-wheel setup** (questions/Playbill/Play Structure); diverges after the pick — at-home lands on the card, no build-a-day.
- So pre-wheel V2 work (reflection page, date logic) ports across; the build-a-day / escape-hatch work is main-only.
- Open question: is at-home a second funnel or a bundled tier? (Affects whether it shares billing/auth.)

## Sequencing — recommendation ("sales first" instinct is right)
1. **Finish the beta** — get to ~10 responses, light polish only.
2. **Choose the pricing model** from that feedback.
3. **Wire Stripe → start charging.** This is the revenue moment, before V2.
4. **Let paying users prioritize V2** — ship themes incrementally based on what people actually ask for; don't big-bang it.
5. **softplay-at-home** is a parallel track — decide funnel vs. bundled tier.

**Bottom line:** V1.5 is basically done. The next milestone isn't V2 — it's **monetization** (beta → pricing → Stripe). V2 is a rolling roadmap funded by early sales, not a gated launch.
