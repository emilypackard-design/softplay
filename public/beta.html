Where we're going
Now

Core planning app

softplay is more than a search or a chatbot. It's a planning companion that offers suggestions through an interface that makes the experience feel less like research and more like play. It's familiar enough to feel like you, surprising enough to get you out of a rut.

Coming

Calendar + community

Date-sensitive events, local happenings, and early community features. Plus playlist sync to get you in the mood, and calendar sync to get you organized.

The dream

Helping you find your place

softplay started as a tool to beat decision fatigue, but the dream is bigger: a travel companion who surprises you with suggestions and gets smarter with every play. When you land somewhere new, you see the spots locals actually love, plus flagged issues like closures before they cost you a Saturday. Real one-time events, shows, and meetups recommended by other families, not by an algorithm. Your Playbill will still remember your crew. The dream is a community that knows everywhere.
